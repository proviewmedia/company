About the Project

Peelr is a digital card game designed to spark real conversations. Instead of small talk, players draw cards with prompts that go deeper — the kind of questions that make people laugh, think, and connect.

We designed and built the experience from concept to product — the game mechanics, the visual identity, and the digital platform. The goal was simple: make it easy for people to have better conversations, whether they're on a date, at a party, or hanging with friends.

What We Delivered

  • Game concept and mechanics
  • Mobile app design
  • Brand identity and visual system
  • Card content and prompt writing
  • UI/UX design
  • Development
