About the Project

Intruity needed a brand identity and website layout that matched the sophistication of their technology. They came to us with a vision but no visual identity — no logo, no design direction, no web presence.

We designed the logo from scratch — creating a mark that feels modern, clean, and credible in the tech space. From there, we laid out the website structure and visual design, giving them a foundation that communicates trust and innovation from the first visit.

What We Delivered

  • Logo design and brand mark
  • Website layout and visual design
  • Brand color palette and typography
  • Design system for consistency
  • Responsive layout across devices
